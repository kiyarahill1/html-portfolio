The Best Movies According to Kiyara

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Gladiator

I used to love watching this movie as a kid. Every now and then I will go back and rewatch.

Remember The Titans

I am a big football fan and I love movies that incorporate that. Remember The Titans was one of my favorites growing up and I will always love it.

Waterboy

This movie always cracks me up when I watch it. To this day I will quote this movie randomly. One of the greatest of all time.
